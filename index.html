Band History

The Monkees are a American rock pop band that were originally concieved in 1965 and became active from 1966 up to present day. Our band originally consisted of Peter Tork, Micky Dolenz, Michael Nesmith, Davy Jones. We released multiple songs and albums throughout the years from 1965 to 2018 and split up and reunited multiple times during that time. We were inspired by the Beetles and got to meet them when we visited them in England. We made appearances on television which included a television series and performed at various events and shows. We were one of the early pioneers of the music video format according to music historians and we paved the way for every boy band in our wake. Our last tour as was in 2016 called our 50th anniversary tour. Unfortunatly due to health reasons we had to cut the tour short but we plan on making up for the lost time.
Today we still perform with remaining members Mikey Dolenz and Michael Nesmith and also do our own solo gigs. We continue to perform today and enjoy doing smaller gigs such as weddings for our fans.

Peter Tork

Peter Tork 1942-2019 was our keyboardist and bass guitarist who helped the Monkees to produce six albums of which fours went to number 1 in the charts. On top of that he helped the band produce some catchy tunes on the piano such as the introduction of the song Day Dream Believer.



Michael Nesmith

Michael Nesmith 1942-present is a band member of the Monkees who helped co-write the Monkees tv special called Hey hey its the Monkees in 1997. He was called the Wool hat guy at his first audition when he showed up on his motorcycle. He contributed both vocally and instrumentally to the Monkees. He has had a solo career too and continues to play today with Micky Dolenz.



Davy Jones

Davy Jones 1945-2012 was a lead vocalist sang lead on many of the Monkees recordings including "I Wanna Be Free" and "Daydream Believer". Davy was around with the band since it's creation and helped us make most recordings except in 1986 and since his passing. One of Jones other talents was acting and he perform in Oliver in the west England. He originally wanted to be a jockey when he left secondary school but his destiny was to sing and to act.



Micky Dolenz

Mickey Dolenz 1945-Present is a band member of the Monkees who is often seen with a big smile who in 1965 became a part of the band and plays the drums and is a lead vocalist. He performs today with Michael Nesmith and like his fellow band members starred in their television show the Monkees. He started his career in show business in 1956. He had is own band prior the Monkees in the early 1960s till he joined the Monkees.
